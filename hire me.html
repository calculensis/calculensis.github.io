hire me

Written on Aug 23, 2022

Are you trying to achieve something good in the world with your organization? Are you struggling with a messy situation?

Or perhaps you're an individual needing help with complex personal decisions, e.g., deciding whether to switch careers?

If either of these is true, then I might be just the one for the job!

I use time-tested methodologies to enable your decision making, particularly to sort out messy situations with a lot of uncertainty involved. Importantly, I am someone you and other stakeholders can be at ease talking to.

For more about me, see the about page; here is what you can expect when working with me:

  • During an initial phone or video consultation (with no fee), I will listen carefully and ask a series of questions to get a clear picture of your situation; from there we can determine if there is a good fit between the issues at hand and the guidance I can offer.

  • If there's indeed a good fit, we will move forward.

Here are the plans I offer:1

Individual plan

You retain me as a personal advisor and we continue to meet virtually 1-4 times a month for an hour each visit. During these sessions I will act as a guide through your decision making process.

$250 per session.

Organizational plan

To the extent that you feel comfortable with it, in addition to collecting relevant information from yourself during the initial consultation, I will contact up to 15 other stakeholders and collect detailed information from them concerning the problematic situation.

After considering carefully these details, I will touch base with you again; we will construct an intervention strategy together. I will direct this intervention remotely with you and the other stakeholders. These sessions will typically last 2-3 hours each and occur over 2-3 days, depending on what approaches are needed.

From $3000 up to $7000 for the entire intervention; half up front.2

Analytics plan

It's often the case that machine learning, which is part of my expertise set, can support key parts of a plan resulting from an intervention. I will analyze data that your organization collects following the intervention and send a written report of the findings.

$250 up to $1000 per report.3

Like what you see? Schedule a consultation.
